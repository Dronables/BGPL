# BGPL
A Better GPL license.
Its a license that prioritizes crediting over sharing.

Its a License made for people who want their work to be credit when its being used by other people.

Its an open-source license so sharing is caring :)

Why BGPL? Existing licenses like GPL and MIT dont handle crediting in a practical or a human way. So I made BGPL to fix this issue, to get everybody's work to be credited by everyone, at the end of the day its their work :D
